About the Project

Because of our ever speedening society, our interest in cultural heritage is declining. Still, there is no reason at all to forget your history, where you come from. Malcolm X rightfully said that

"A man without any history is like a tree without roots"
Hopefully this platform can encourage people to remember the past and to enjoy exporing it.
