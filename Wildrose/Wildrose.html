Review

  So far, we have laid our focus on artefacts; cultural items we traditionally connotate with a museum. But our culture is, in fact, much more than what museums cover.

  We have covered many traditional pieces of culture, but when I saw a screening of Wild Rose the other day I knew it deserved a place on this website.

Wild Rose (2018)

As country as
can be

Cultural Impact

   Wild Rose follows a soulsearching young mother choosing between caring for her kids and chasing dreams to be a Country Music star. Its authentic portrayal of country music and pure quality makes it a prime example why it should stand side by side with other cultural artefacts.

The Story

   Rose-Lynn is a feisty young mother: she is impulsive and wants to do things purely her way. But, sadly enough, this tands to stand in the way of her kids's care. Her vibrant dream and golden voice offcast the grey scales of living a typical life in the industrial city of Glasgow.

Cinematography
